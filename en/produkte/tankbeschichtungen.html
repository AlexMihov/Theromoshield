Tankshield Climate-Shield®

Tankshield Climate-Shield®

‹ › ×

    Description

    Climate-Shield® Tank Shield is a highly efficient, energy-saving, flexible coating, designed to protect and lower the external surface temperature of storage vessels. It is non-toxic and friendly to the environment. It is completely washable and resists many harsh chemicals and dirt pick-up. This water-based coating is made from an acrylic resin system filled with vacuumed ceramic micro spheres that acts as a thermally efficient blanket.
    Climate-Shield® Tank Shield has superb fire resistance - it is grade Class A by UL Standards. It can be obtained in a variety of architectural colors for signs and logo making. It leaves a beautiful matt finish but can be made glossy when coated with Climate-Shield® Clear Coat.

    Typical use

    For the exterior of steel petroleum storage tanks and pipelines where the reduction of surface temperature is desired. Used also for the exterior of steel or polymer water tanks, grain storage silos and the exterior of most chemical storage tanks. This coating is resistant to 26 different harsh chemicals including 20% hydrochloric and 25% sulfuric acids.

    Surface preparation

    All surfaces must be clean and free from rust, dust, dirt, oil or grease. Minimally, surfaces must be cleaned to remove any loose or chipped paint or any other foreign material prior to the application of Tank Shield. Rust should be controlled with the use of rust inhibitors.

    Additional information

    Colors

    White and any custom color available. Darker colors will give a correspondingly lower Reflectivity.

    • Primer

      Use alkyd or epoxy quality primers where required and/or rust inhibitors to make sure rust will not be present during the application of the coating.

    • Theoretical coverage (2-3 coats)

      7m² per gallon at 380 microns DFT (75 ft²/gallon at 12 mills DFT) with no loss

    • Drying time

      At 75°F (25°C) and 50% relative humidity:

      • To set: 45 minutes
      • To recoat: 12 hours
      • To through: 12 hours

    • Adhesion

      Excellent adhesion to steel and a wide variety of substrates: wood, urethane foam, galvanized steel, aluminum, concrete, asbestos and many others.

    • Mixing

      Stir each container thoroughly using low speed mechanical agitation to avoid air entrapment.

    • Verdünnung

      Es ist keine Verdünning nötig, jedoch können kleine Mengen Wasser (0.24 Liter pro 3.8L bzw. 1 cup/gal.) hinzugefügt werden um die Verdunstung welche entstehen könnte zu kompensieren.
      Vorsicht: Zu grosse Verdünnung kann zu schlechteren Resultaten führen.

    • Equipment

      Roller or airless spray application is recommended. Very small areas may be brushed. When Thermo-Shield® Tank Shield is applied by brush, three coats are required for adequate protection.
      Airless Sprayer: Apply all coats in the same direction. Do not cross spray. Cross spraying may reveal surface imperfections such as small undulations, etc.

      • Tip orfice: 0.0517 oder 0.515 inch (minimum)
      • Atomizing pressure: 2000 psi (140 bar)
      • Fan spread: 60°
      • Pump: min 0.5 gal/min (2 L/min)
      Prime pump with water before attempting to spray Climate-Shield® Interior Wall. Clean tools and equipment with warm soapy water. Rinse with clean water; flush mineral spirits through spray equipment to prevent rusting and to lubricate packing and gaskets.

    • Packaging

      Climate-Shield® Tankshield is available in:

      • 5 gal (19 L) pails, 50lbs (22.7 kg)
      • 55 gal (209 L) drums, 600lbs (272 kg)

    • Storage

      The product has a shelf life of 2 years.
      Minimum 32°F (1°C)
      Maximum 86°F (30°C)
      DON'T FREEZE
