Metalprimer Climate-Shield®

Metalprimer Climate-Shield®

‹ › ×

    Description

    Climate-Shield® Metal Primer is rust inhibitive, water-based, high quality, 100% acrylic resin, corrosion resistant coating.

    Typical uses

    It is used as a Primer / Base Coat on all rusty metal tanks & pipelines, water tanks, silos, storage bins, metal roofs, beams etc., surfaces, prior to the application of Climate-Shield® Roof Coat or Tank Shield.

    Additional information

    Approximate Coverage (minimum 2 coats)

    150 – 250 ft² / Gallon (3.7- 6.0 m² / Liter) - per coat

    • Drying time

      At 75°F (25°C) and 50% relativen humidity:

      • To touch: 1 hour
      • To recoat: 4 hours

    • Packaging

      • 5 gal (19 L) pails
      • 55 gal (209 L) drums
