    Presentation of the products



    Presentation
    of the products

    Climate-Shield® is an elastomeric water-based pure acrylic resin system, filled with hollow, vacuumed, sodium borosilicate, ceramic, micro spheres. Climate-Shield® is made of totally synthetic state-of-the-art materials, so it is not subject to rapid breakdown like traditional coatings. All of the ingredients are synthetic. Most other paints and coatings use organic materials (natural ingredients) that are broken down by nature by things like thermal shock (extreme heat or cold), ultra violet light, ozone decay, wind, rain, snow, hail, sand abrasion, chemicals, dirt and air pollution. Other paints and coatings fade, get hard, crack, peel, decay, turn chalky and crusty or simply just decompose. Climate-Shield®’s unique ingredients overcome all these problems.
